Task 7 - Identify and Remove Suspicious Browser Extensions
===========================================================

Procedure:
1. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.).
2. Go to the Extensions/Add-ons Manager:
   - Chrome: Menu (⋮) → More Tools → Extensions
   - Firefox: Menu (☰) → Add-ons and Themes → Extensions
3. Review all installed extensions carefully.
4. Check the following for each extension:
   - Name and developer
   - Permissions requested
   - Reviews and ratings
5. Identify suspicious or unused extensions:
   - Unknown or untrusted developer
   - Excessive permissions (e.g., read all browsing history)
   - Poor reviews or flagged as malicious
6. Remove suspicious/unnecessary extensions:
   - Chrome: Click "Remove"
   - Firefox: Click "Remove"
7. Restart your browser and check performance improvements.
8. Research how malicious extensions can harm users.
9. Document:
   - List of removed extensions
   - Reasons for removal
   - Security lessons learned
10. Save this procedure, readme, and report to GitHub.
